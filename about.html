About the Yale School of Art

The Yale School of Art is one of the leading art schools in the world, offering master's degrees in graphic design, painting and printmaking, photography, and sculpture. Our program nurtures innovative artists through a commitment to creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our History

Established in 1869, the Yale School of Art has been at the forefront of art education for over 150 years. The school has produced some of the most influential artists, designers, and photographers in the world.
